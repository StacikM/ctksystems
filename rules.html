Section B

Punishment Scheme

  1. Warning A formal CTK warning lasting 30 days or indefinitely. Two active warnings escalate to the next level.

  3. Temporary Suspension Loss of access to CTK projects (excluding partner services) for up to one year.
  4. CTK Blacklist Permanent loss of access to all CTK projects, including partner projects. Generally final and rarely lifted.

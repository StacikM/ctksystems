Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions:

Q1: What is CTK?

A1: CTK is an international helping system for your Discord server. We may sell sometimes some products like donation, but for now everything is free!

Q2: What is a CTK blacklist?

A1: An CTK blacklist is the worst punishment you can get from CTK. You can't join any CTK servers or that have CTK bots added.

Q3: What does CTK stand for?

A1: Good question! CTK stands for CTaciK.

Important answers:

Q1: Why did a CTK bot DM me/or a member about being banned from some features of CTK?

A1: If you or your member violates our Terms of services (https://ctksystem.com/rules), we will have to penalize him from your server in terminating some features. In a emergency, we can ban someone if they are abusing CTK's services.


A2: CTK can remove features from a member and notifying the member with a DM saying the features that have been removed and why and who to contact to appeal or for questions. The server owner can't sadly undo these punishments until they contact CTK.

Q2: Does CTK admins have admin commands on my server?

A1: In some point yes and in some point no, CTK admins have access to all bot commands provided by CTK even that are for your mods. CTK admins do not have administrator permission on your server to change anything.

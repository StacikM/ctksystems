Privacy Policy

Effective: 21 October 2025 · Last updated: 11 June 2026

1. Introduction

We value your privacy. This policy explains how CTK collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect
Data TypePurpose
IP AddressesUsed for security enforcement only
Usernames & User IDsIdentity management within CTK Systems, it may be transported to different platforms for logs or moderation.
3. How We Use Your Information

Data is used solely for security enforcement and identity management within CTK services.

4. Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using Firebase or/and JSON with strong access controls and protections in place.

5. Data Sharing
  • We do not sell your data.
  • Data is shared only if required by law or for user protection purposes.
  • Limited information may be shared with Google Ads if a project includes advertising.
6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete (except punishments), and object to your data. Contact us at stacik@ctksystem.com to exercise these rights.

7. Punishment Data

Punishment records are not deleted unless the punishment was issued in error.

CTK Blacklist data stored: IP address, Discord username, email (if registered), reason, date, and moderator. This data cannot be requested for deletion for security reasons.

Feature ban data stored: Discord username, feature removed, reason, date, moderator, and project ID. This may be deleted upon a general data deletion request — attempting to exploit a deletion to bypass an active punishment will result in a CTK Blacklist.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time and will post changes to this page.

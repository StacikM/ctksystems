Welcome to CTK. We are dedicated to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our services, including our Discord server management and game creation activities.

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously. The information we collect is stored securely using Firebase and other trusted services. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccuracies in the information we have about you.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at stacik@ctksystem.com.
